Sicilian Games

What began as a small fitness initiative a few years ago has grown into Ahmedabad’s most awaited entrepreneurial sporting extravaganza. The Sicilian Games bring together 3,000+ members to compete in 18+ sports, representing their chapters in a month-long tournament to win the coveted Sicilian Cup. It’s the only event where the city’s business leaders swap boardrooms for playing fields, showcasing not just their competitive spirit, but their ability to collaborate, lead, and inspire through sport.

Over the years, the Games have been hosted at some of the city’s top venues, from Adani Shantigram and Belvedere Club to Adani Sportsline, KN Sports Arena, Ahmedabad Racquet Academy, and the iconic Riverfront for the Night River Run.

Each edition has raised the bar, both in scale and experience, making the Games a cultural milestone in Ahmedabad’s business community.
